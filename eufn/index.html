--- layout: page-portfolio-project title: "EUFN" short-name: eufn client: "The European Union Families Network" site-url: "eufn.eu - Currently offline" works: Site design & build / logo design --- {% include JB/setup %} The European Union Families Network (Reseau Famillie de L'Union European) is a volunteer organisation setup and funded by the [EU External Action Service](http://www.eeas.europa.eu/index_en.htm "EU External Action Service"). EUFN is tasked to provide support to the spouses of EU diplomats and staff as they take up residence at new diplomatic delegations or return to Brussels. #####Project details##### * Fully responsive * Minimal page weight to ensure fast loading on slower internet connections. Broadband *is not* standard in deepest Africa. * Multi-ligual support for all content on both the front and back ends. * A way for members to connect to share their experiences from around the world. * A paid subscription membership system with private content. * An events system for EUFN social and training in Brussels. * A new logo for use in any language or culture.
