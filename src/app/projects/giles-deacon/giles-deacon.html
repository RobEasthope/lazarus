Giles Deacon


Client: Giles Deacon | Agency: FST

Giles Deacon is a British fashion designer, best known for his playful designs and his collaboration with High Street retailers. I was contracted by FST to help design and build the front end of the Gile’s new portfolio and public relations site.

Technical details

The Giles site was built using the Foundation front end framework and ‘Waterfall’ layouts was handled by the Wookmark.js library. On the back end the site was built with the Laravel PHP framework.
