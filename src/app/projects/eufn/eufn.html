EUFN


Client: Reseau Familiale de L'Union Europeene

EUFN is a European Union funded organisation providing advice and support to EU diplomatic personnel and their families while working overseas or in Brussels.

The brief was to build to provide full-stack development services to build a Wordpress site with a membership system, private content for members, and Paypal billing integration.
