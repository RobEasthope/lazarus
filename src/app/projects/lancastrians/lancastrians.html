Lancastrians in London


Client: The Association of Lancastrians in London

The back story

The Association of Lancastrians in London is a venerable social group bringing together residents of the Duchy of Lancaster from across the Greater London area.

The Lancastrians Crest

For several years the Association had been using a smaller version of their logo for print work. The Associations switch to digital required a higher quality logo so I designed a brand new logo using historically accurate heraldic elements.

Years later this crest is still in use and was recently used in print magazine, Country Life.

The Association site

The Association site was designed as a fully responsive Wordpress site built using Bootstrap 2. It is used to give an alternative way for members to view the quarterly association newsletter, while giving information on upcoming events, and ways for members to share memories.
