Layla Moran Campaign


Client: Layla Moran

Layla Moran was the Liberal Democrat prospective parliamentary candidate for Oxford West & Abingdon during the 2015 UK General Election.

As part of her internal party selection campaign I helped design her branding and built several iterations website for the General, Greater London Assembly, and internal party elections.

Oxford West & Abingdon campaign site

The Oxford West & Abingdon campaign was a bespoke Wordpress site providing information for volunteers, blogs from the candidate, and multimedia content.
