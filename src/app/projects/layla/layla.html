The back story

Layla Moran was the Liberal Democrat prospective parliamentary candidate for Oxford West & Abingdon during the 2015 UK General Election.

Over three years I helped develop her branding and built several iterations of her website for the 2010 General Election, 2012 Greater London Assembly elections, and 2012 internal party PPC selections.

Campaign site

The Oxford West & Abingdon campaign was a bespoke Wordpress site providing information for volunteers, blogs articles, video content and ways to donate.
