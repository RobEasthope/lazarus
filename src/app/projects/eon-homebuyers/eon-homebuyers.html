E.ON Homebuyers Guide


Client: e.on | Agency: Bisqit

The back story

The e.on Homebuyers Guide is a responsive microsite offering new home owners advice on possible energy efficiency savings and money-saving deals their new property.

Built, tested, and seeded with content in four weeks, the project is currently awaiting deployment to production.

Technical details

  • Dynamic jQuery app
  • Fully responsive structure built with Foundation for Sites
  • Front end tooling handled by Gulp
  • Slider functionality provided by Owl Carousel
