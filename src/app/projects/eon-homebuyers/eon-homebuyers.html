E.ON Homebuyers Guide


Client: e.on | Agency: Bisqit

Demo site

The back story

The e.on homebuyers guide is a small microsites offering homebuyers advice on energy efficiency savings on their new property.

Technical details

The Giles site was built using the Foundation front end framework and ‘Waterfall’ layouts was handled by the Wookmark.js library. On the back end the site was built with the Laravel PHP framework.
