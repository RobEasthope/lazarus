Haygarth


Client: Haygarth | Agency: Haygarth

The Haygarth project was a full stack redesign of Haygarth’s company portfolio and blog. The brief was to build a fully responsive site which showcased Haygarth’s creative work.

Backing this up the old Drupal based CMS was stripped out and replaced with a bespoke Laravel CMS built around simplicity and ease of use.
