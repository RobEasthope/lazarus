Ambr.


Client: Ambr Media

Ambr media was a short lived media company specialising in websites, PR and graphic design. Ambr was seeded with money from The Prince's Trust and clients included charities and finance companies.

The logo

Following a successful launch Ambr embarked on a full redesign of it's logo and branding. The initial work experimented with using painted watercolour swatches overlayed with typography.
