The back story

Arup was a pitch prototype for the redesign of arup.com. Making extensive use subtle animation to set itself apart apart from the competition and combined with an ambitious backend, TH_NK ultimately successfully won the pitch.

Technical details

  • Animation scrolling handled by ScrollMagic.js
  • Greensock GSAP animations
  • Slick carousel
  • Gulp.js front-end tooling
