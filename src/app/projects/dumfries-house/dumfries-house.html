Dumfries House


Client: Dumfries House | Agency: FST

The back story

Dumfries House is a Palladian country house in set in 2000 acres of countryside outside Ayrshire, Scotland. In 2013 I was involved in the opening stages of their website redesign restructuring content and doing UIX planning.

The brief

The existing content was spread over three different sites leading to confusion and missed opportunities to tell visitors of what the Dumfries Estate had to offer.
