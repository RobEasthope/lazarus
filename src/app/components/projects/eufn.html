European Union Families Network

The European Union Families Network (Reseau Famillie de L'Union European) is a volunteer organisation setup and funded by the EU External Action Service. EUFN is tasked to provide support to the spouses of EU diplomats and staff as they take up residence at new diplomatic delegations or return to Brussels.
