--- layout: page-portfolio-project title: "The Association of Lancastrians in London" short-name: lancastrians client: "The Association of Lancastrians in London" site-url: "www.lancastriansinlondon.org.uk" works: Site design & build / logo design --- {% include JB/setup %} The Association of Lancastrians in London is a social club bringing together members from the [Duchy of Lancaster](http://www.duchyoflancaster.co.uk/) for regular meet ups and tours. The Association requested a new website and logo to both publise the organisations and to provide an easier way to communicate with it's membership. #####The Lancastrian Crest##### In keeping with the Associations history the new crest displays the arms of the Duchy of Lancaster: The fluer-de-lis introduced by Kind Edward III and the lion of Richard the Lionheart.
